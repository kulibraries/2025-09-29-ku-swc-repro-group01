# World-famous biscuits and gravy

Serves 6 as written (typically need 20 servings per hour of service)

## Biscuits

### Ingredients

+ 195g all-purpose flour
+ 195g cake flour
+ 24g baking powder
+ 1 1/2 tsp. sugar
+ 1 1/2 tsp. salt
+ 170g butter
+ 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
+ Love


## Gravy

### Ingredients

+ 450g sausage
+ 50g butter
+ medium onion
+ 1 1/2 tsp salt
+ 70g all-purpose flour
+  3 cups milk
+ Love
