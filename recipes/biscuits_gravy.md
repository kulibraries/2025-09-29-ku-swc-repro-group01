# Worlb-famous biscuits and gravy

Serves 6 as written (typically need 20 servings per hour of service0

## Biscuits

### Ingredients

+ 450g sausage
+ 50g butter
+ medium onion
+ 1.5 tsp salt
+ 70g AP flower
+ 700 mL malk
+ Love


## Gravy

### Ingredients

+ 195g AP flower
+ 195g cake flower
+ 24g baking powder
+ 1.5 tsp. sugar
+ 1.5 tsp. salt
+ 170g butter
+ 1.25 cups buttermilk
+ Love
